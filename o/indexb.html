Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

This morning, please welcome, smoothly, The Jazzment.
Most of us stick to some artists over the years, coming back listening to them for they carry some special feeling and memories. That's the case for me with The Jazzment. Their relaxing flow put me at peace, hope they'll carry you on the same vibes this morning.

The _Cool & Smooth_ Jazzment

Three albums released on theirbandcamp page that can follow you everywhere. With calm, clarity, the dutch duo wrap you in a perfect and delightful mood. Sit snugly with a good cup of coffee and listen to this selection they're sharing with us.

Enjoy their morning selection

track1

"Probably hard to believe, but besides us there are some other dope producers representing the Netherlands. Personally we find Henning one of the illest, bringing back that West-Coast G-funk sound. He definitely has the smoothest beats of the country."

track2

"This man has been one of the main inspirations to us since the beginning.
People like Freddie Joachim, Dilla and Pete are among the producers we admire the most. Waves is just one of his many classic beats, often characterized by a mellow guitar."

track3

"Man, this song is so dope. We came across Overdoz. like two years ago and Lauren London became our summer anthem for 2012. This song was playing all day while we were chilling to the fullest. Just bump this shit on sunday and enjoy."

That's all folks! A big thank to The Jazzment for their selection.
Thanks for listening. Enjoy your sunday!
