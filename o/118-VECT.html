Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

This morning, VECT selected some dope beats inspiring us a very greeny MailTape. Go with the flow, let your heavy mind evaporates and your body feel the groove. Suddenly, a shaft of sunlight diffracted by a drip of coffee floating in the air opens a door. Let's trip together.

VECT, a guy who tends to infinity.

We're in Toulouse, France. The guy, Emile Sacré, aka VECT, evolves in a multi-dimensional universe. Motiongraphics, design, music, this folk never stop and tries to maintain one common point between all these disciplines : Quality. I remember having discovered him by watching one of his great videos on his vimeo. Digging in his portfolio I then found the beginning of VECT : beats with a soul. Altitudes, his album, does exactly what says its title. It pushes you to higher states of mind. His compositions are very visually inspired. Each beat morphs into a pixel forming altogether beautiful pictures. Can't wait to listen to his next one.

Let the trip begins with his selection.

Knx. - Plaiurprt

"Here is all I love in a track ! An intense warm feeling mixing R'n'b and soulful, but with a rough touch !"

Mr Carmack - Say Yes Or Say No (Aaliyah Flip)

"This downtempo remix of Aaliyah is very aerial. Kicks are floating randomly along the track with a subtle bass played off-beat resulting in a high quality production. Something very modern, indeed futuristic."

Miles Bonny - Clap Clap

"A dynamic track which offers another way of playing the original track by the The Meters from the 70's. Miles Bonny is singer, producer and jazzist. You can notice a perfect groove coming from the dubbed voice, an acoustic beat and dry guitar riffs."

That’s all for today folks. Thanks to VECT and Laurène Berchoteau for the picture. Time to get back on earth and have an amazing sunday.
