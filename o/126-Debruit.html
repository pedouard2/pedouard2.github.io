Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

This morning, discover Débruit who’s known for his eclecticism and tireless will to create mindblowing tracks. I’ve got to say, some tracks might not sound so "easy" but if you manage to open your ears... well you might feel new sensations. Close your eyes and let’s flow together.

Trippy, unique and creative.

“Singularity”, is one of the first word that pops in my mind when trying to write about Débruit’s music. I don’t know anything similar. Of course, we could think of Hudson Mohawke but still, the guy keeps surprising me every time. To be fully honest, I sometime need to give a few tries in order to really feel his sound. Imagine being lost in the middle of an african village with Dali-esque landscapes around, now you drop in the center a london club shouting hip-hop bass and you let the sun stretch your mind like a kid playing with Play-Doh.

Africa and harmonica pulsates his sunday morning.
