Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

This morning, let's start with dreamy vibes selected by Christoph El Truento from New-Zealand. I've then spiced it up with some vitamins resulting in a colorful music story, from slow-motion sunrise on the beach to magic landscapes and islands metamorphosed into dancefloors.

So many talents on this little island

I'm never bored of digging into New Zealand's music. Almost every time I look in this direction, I find some gold. Amazing how this island feeds the inspiration of its creative inhabitants. Christoph El Truento is no exception to this, his latest album remains one of the best I had the chance to discover last year. His boundless creativity astonishes me every time as much as his ability to teleport his listeners in his world. Many musicians try to create a "new sound" but rare are the ones who manage to produces something which is both revolutionary and subtle enough to take its auditors by the hand, inviting us to let ourselves connect to a new state of flow.

His dreamy selection

track1

"This is actually my girlfriends favourite song right now, she showed it to me and it's grown on me over the last few weeks. Some really beautiful tones and push n pull style rhythms in this piece of music. Can take you to that other place if you listen right."

track2

"I guess this was another one that my lady put me on to, we are really into the moonglyph catalogue and I woke up one morning to her playing it from my laptop and I was on a good one for the rest of the day because of it. Something in that guitar line and tone that gives me a certain feeling I can't describe. Sounds like something from my past, sad and happy at the same time."

track3

"Discovered this one through the yoga records catalogue. I recently purchased all the music from their website. I always love a nice song like this to kick off the day, something really well written and structured, this is a pretty lil number. Doesn't need to be played too loud. I think I was maybe a folk musician in another life."

That's all folks! A big thank to Christoph for his selection, don't hesitate to check out his album. Enjoy your sunday and feel the love.
