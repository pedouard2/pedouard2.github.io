Our story

Overflowed with information, it's became very hard to find new talended artists. Of course there is Pandora and the like but with such a quantity of music out there now, it's easy to end up with a background music consumed like disposable tissues.

Being truly passionate about music, we wanted to find a way to help enthusiasts discover new music in an easy and consistent way.
That's why we created the MailTape. Every sunday morning, we send you an eclectic selection of heart-touching songs curated in collaboration with the most inspiring artists. You may not like everything you hear, but if you make at least one discovery then our job is done.

Created in 2011, more than 100 artists have been featured on MailTape, such as Nicolas Jaar, Connan Mockasin, Gonzalez, Wax Tailor, Shigeto. There are many more to come.
We hope you'll enjoy this experience. Now sit back, relax and listen...

12 days have already passed since new year. Remember all the good things you said you wanted to improve this year ? Time to get on and dare to be better with this episode full of empowering vibes. Welcome our first guest of the year, Nitemoves. His memorable selection is the sign 2014 is going to be full of life. Ready ? Let's go !

Nitemoves ?

I'm a big fan of Tycho and Com Truise. So when I found out about Nitemoves, aka Rory O'Connor, who's known for supporting them on tour, as a drummer, I had to listen to his productions. "Themes" has been a beautiful discovery and I'd put it easily in the best albums I've discovered last year. Perfect for dreaming, his music, whether it's vibrant or more relaxed, is always deep and invites the listener to fly above beautiful landscapes.

3 tracks he picked with his heart

Ptaki - Krystyna

"I love these disco edits, they're so mesmerizing. This guy ptaki does amazing reworks of old Polish disco. Wait for the diva voxs ;)"

Halide - 052611

"I find myself dropping it into dj sets a lot, love the energy on this track. Such a great melody, it just kind of rolls forward."

Gold Panda - If U Knew (Reprise Long Live Take)

"For me one of the most beautiful tracks of 2013. I could listen to this on a loop for quite some time. That melancholy kora sample slays me every time."

That's all folks! Be alive and have a great sunday. Cheers.
